The Room Breakdown

For this video I wanted to make a creepy looking room with lights flickering and with some strange noises to give off a Horror vibe.

To make this I first started with modeling all the diffrent objects like the bed,dresser, heater and all the other little details then i took this objects to substance painter to texture them, after which I went back to blender to set up the scene in a visualy pleasing manner, added some spooky backlight animate the hand and the filckering lights and its time to render. I rendered it in layers and took those layers into davinci resolve to finsh of the project.
