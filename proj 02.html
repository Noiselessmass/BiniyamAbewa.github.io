Coca Cola Breakdown

On this project I tried to achive a professional advertisement look with simple background and well placed lighting

For this animation everything was all done in blender where I modeled and textured the can and the ice, I also used blender's fluid simulator to make the cokes splashing effect on the cans. Finally I added the lights and the camera and renderd the scene. After which i used blenders compositor to add some final touches and its DONE.
