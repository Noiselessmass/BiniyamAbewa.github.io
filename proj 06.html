Math Breakdown

This is a 2d animation for kids to teach them basic math in amharic language

for this animation I started out by making a concept video in photoshop after that I made the different assets like the numbers, the teacher and his expressions, I then exported them as transparent png and imported them into davinci resolve to compile the different images into an animation. after that i added some sound effects and finshed off the project.
