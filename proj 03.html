Ship in a Bottle Breakdown

Here I tried to capture the mesmerizing 'ship in a bottle' in a short video

For this project i modeled the bottle and the body of the ship in blender, used bezier curves to make the ropes and cloth simulation to make the sails of the ships, I then prceeded to texture the entire scene. all in blender of course. then I added the lights, animated the camera and off we go to render everything. I added some finshing touches in the compositor to finsh it of. and we have our short animation.
